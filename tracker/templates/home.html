About Mind Guard

Mind Guard is an AI-powered mental health support application designed to provide parents with real-time insights into their child's mental well-being. By analyzing online browsing data, Mind Guard detects patterns that may indicate signs of depression or other mental health concerns.

Our mission is to empower parents with the tools they need to proactively support their children's mental health. Mind Guard offers a privacy-focused solution with detailed reporting, real-time monitoring, and alerts via various channels, including WhatsApp, email, SMS, and SOS.

We also offer the option to include doctors in alert notifications, ensuring that professional support is readily available when needed.
